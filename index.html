What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where students individually or collaboratively create innovative solutions with technology, often using hardware, software, or both! Students present and share their ideas with other participants and can be rewarded with prizes for their work. In addition, due to many workshops and expereinced mentors, a hackathon is a great learning oppurtunity and experience. Overall, hackathons are an excellent event for any student with an interest in computer science and programming to attend, no matter the skill level.

Innovate

An idea is the foundation of any hack. Be creative!

Develop
